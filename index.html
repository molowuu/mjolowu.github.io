Portfolio · Designed & Shipped

This Portfolio

Designed with Claude · Developed and deployed on GitHub.

Overview

Why I built this

I wanted a portfolio that felt simple, clean, and intentionally mine — not a template with my name swapped in. I explored platforms like Google Sites, Wix, and Framer, but found them too rigid. I tried pre-built templates, but editing someone else's design felt unintuitive and limiting. Building from scratch was the clearest path to getting exactly what I wanted — and an opportunity to treat this as a real product, learning by doing along the way.

Process

How it came together

The biggest challenge was the iteration. I went through multiple rounds of layout, structure, colour, and content before landing on something that felt right — simple, modern, and not overcrowded. I used ChatGPT as a thinking partner to talk through ideas and clarify my direction, then translated those into prompts for Claude, which guided the design and build. It was a back-and-forth process that pushed me to make deliberate decisions at every step — from the split layout to the card carousel to the choice of white over beige. The final site was version-controlled and deployed on GitHub, making it easy to update and maintain going forward.
